// ABOUT THE GAME

BUILT ON
CHAOS.
REFINED
BY PLAY.

Source is a 2D sandbox platformer that draws from the anarchic spirit of Garry's Mod— ragdolls, props, contraptions, and absolute mayhem—distilled into a tight side-scrolling world.

Every level is a physics playground. Every character is fully moddable. The workshop is your canvas.

  • ◈ Physics-driven ragdoll characters
  • ◈ Fully open workshop & modding SDK
  • ◈ Procedural destruction system
  • ◈ Co-op & versus multiplayer
